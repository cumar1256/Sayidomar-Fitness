Frequently Asked Questions

Are there sign-up cancellation or contract termination penalties?

No. Our Basic and Elite membership tracks are engineered on rolling month-to-month continuous billing structures. You can terminate auto-renewals 10 business days clear of your next scheduled calendar billing block without encountering exit penalty assessments.

What equipment arrays are permanently provisioned on-site?

Our infrastructure holds pristine multi-station competition compound platforms, solid calibrated steel Olympic plates, specialized multi-grip bars, specialized dual-cable towers, high-performance mechanical air bikes, rowers, and solid dumbbells mapping scales up to 150 lbs.

Can I book one-on-site orientation tours prior to selecting memberships?

Absolutely. Reach through our core Contact page configuration to set clean time blocks with one of our master training coordinators to review the gear deployment systems first-hand.
